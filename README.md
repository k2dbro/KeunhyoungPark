## 🔍 Research

| Area | What I do |
|---|---|
| **ML-based damage detection** | Neural-network and interval-based frameworks that detect and localize structural damage without labeled damage data |
| **3D-printed concrete** | Material testing, FE modeling, and capacity prediction of printed concrete structures — incl. NASA's 3D-Printed Mars Habitat Challenge (Penn State team) |
| **Computational structural engineering** | Seismic retrofit optimization, building response monitoring, analysis/design tools |

**Tools:** Python · MATLAB · OpenSees · ABAQUS · Autodesk Nastran
