## Research Highlights

### 🏗️ Machine Learning for Structural Health Monitoring
*Ph.D. research, Georgia Tech*

I develop neural-network frameworks that detect and localize structural damage **without labeled damage data** — the key obstacle in real-world monitoring. My interval-based approach learns the response range of a healthy structure and flags measurements that fall outside it.

<img src="images/shm_result.png" width="600" alt="Damage localization result on benchmark structure">

*Example figure: predicted response intervals vs. measured response, with damage flagged at sensor 7.*

### 🖨️ 3D-Printed Concrete Structures
*M.S. research, Penn State — NASA 3D-Printed Mars Habitat Challenge*

As part of Penn State's NASA Centennial Challenge team, I performed structural design, FE modeling, and strength testing of printed concrete elements — including a sub-scale dome structure ([Frontiers in Built Environment, 2026](https://doi.org/10.3389/fbuil.2026.1854031)).
